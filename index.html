Most lapses are preventable.
A bond cancellation or expired workers comp policy can trigger an automatic suspension within 24 hours. You won't get a notice. By the time you find out, you've usually already lost a job. Reinstatement takes time and paperwork you don't have bandwidth for. Bonded catches it first.
