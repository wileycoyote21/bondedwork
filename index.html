A lapse you don't know about is the expensive kind.
Bond and workers comp lapses can trigger an automatic suspension within 24 hours. By the time you find out — usually at a job site or during a bid review — the damage is done. Reinstatement means fees, paperwork, and weeks of downtime while your phone stops ringing.

Bonded doesn't manage your compliance. It makes sure you always know where you stand — and points you to the right place to act when something comes up.
How it works
1
Enter your license number
We use it to look up your publicly available license, bond, and workers comp status directly from the state database. Nothing more is required.
2
We check your status every day
Status changes can appear within 24 hours of a lapse. We run a daily check against your last known status and flag any difference immediately.
3
You get notified — with a clear path forward
Text or email when your status changes or expiration approaches. Every alert includes a direct link to your license record and the right contact or form to address it — no searching, no guessing.

Bonded is a monitoring and notification service. All data is sourced from publicly available state licensing databases and reflects what is on record at the time of each check.
